# Mora

Mora helps adult intermediate language learners turn their own natural speech into focused, memorable feedback.

## Core experience

- Record up to 30 minutes of natural speech in the target language.
- Analyze native audio directly, without an STT or transcription pipeline.
- Prioritize grammar and vocabulary feedback that will be useful in future speech.
- Release no more than 20 new corrections each day to keep the workload bounded.
- Teach corrections through contextual productive recall and short lessons.

## Success

Learners can notice, recall, and reuse high-value corrections from speech they actually produced without having to read or manage a transcript.

## Non-goals for v1

- Live AI conversation
- Full transcripts
- Pronunciation scoring
- Accounts or social features
- Cloud sync
